Frutinon's story begins with a desire by two young people to create a brand with the identity of their native country and that would be easily differentiated from other brands on the market. Based on this desire, the two decided to study which segment of the market they could enter and make a difference. They quickly realized that in a tropical country with hot temperatures throughout the year, there was a great demand for refreshing drinks.
That was how Frutinon was founded, a company that produces and sells soft drinks and natural juices, yet this was not something new in the market where there are already large companies, however it was natural that they realized that the way of being different was to use the fruits that the country has in abundance.
Then the company started using more characteristic fruits from the country itself, giving the population a different option with a flavor with which they were already familiar. And so the name Frutinon came up, "our fruit", our flavor.
